About

I build for the person on the other side of the screen.

I was born in Mexico and moved to the United States at age four, growing up near El Paso, Texas. I came up through Kinesiology — earning both a B.S. and M.S. at UT Austin, researching how the body responds to stress and change. What stuck wasn't the exercise science. It was the deeper question underneath it: what actually makes people change their behavior, and what gets in the way?

That question has driven every product I've built. At Muuv Labs I scaled a consumer fitness platform to 2M+ global users by obsessing over what kept people coming back — running 200+ A/B experiments, building personalization systems, and managing connected hardware products across iOS, Android, and wearables. At Vi I led a global expansion across 29+ languages and an Apple Fitness+ integration that brought in 139K+ new users.

In 2024 I joined U.S. Soccer Federation to build a referee technology ecosystem from scratch — no product team, no established process, just a complex stakeholder landscape and a mandate to modernize. I came in as a team of one and built the infrastructure from the ground up.

Now I'm developing a parallel practice in AI-powered product tooling — building working prototypes that automate the slow, manual parts of product work. I'm interested in what happens when a PM can move at the speed of the insight, not the speed of the process.
